My First Week at AkiraChix

When I arrived at AkiraChix, it was night, and I was starving due to fasting on Friday. I couldn't eat the chicken on the plane. However, I was warmly welcomed by the alumni and Nancy, which made me feel happy to be in this amazing tech and women-empowering environment. When I shared my hunger challenge with Nancy, she kindly offered me rice, and I ate, took a shower, and went to sleep.

The next morning, I was excited to explore the area and enjoyed spending time with my friends. A few days after my arrival, we started classes. I was surprised to learn that classes began at 7 AM, and waking up early was a bit shocking at first. However, after two days, I adapted to the schedule and managed my time better, waking up earlier with ease.

Overall, I felt happy about my journey here at AkiraChix. Now, I am in my second week, and I am looking forward to what is ahead.

Highlights of My Week

  1. Met inspiring mentors and fellow students.
  2. Participated in engaging assignments.
  3. Gained valuable insights into the tech industry.

Challenges I Faced

  • Adjusting to a new learning environment.
  • Adjusting the new time scedule.
  • Getting acustomed to unfamilar foods.
